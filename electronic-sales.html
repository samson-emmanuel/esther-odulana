Company Overview

Terry Gerry is an electronics retail company operating multiple stores across different countries and states.

Problem Statement

Technical Approach

ETL & Modeling

  • Imported CSV & PDF data from Procurement, CX, and Sales departments.
  • Extracted unique Calendar table from Sales data.
  • Built One-to-Many Star Schema relationships using Power Pivot.
  • Defined Primary Keys in Product/Store tables and Foreign Keys in Sales table.

Analysis & Calculations

  • Used Pivot Tables for high-level aggregated data views.
  • DAX Measure (Orders): Created using DISTINCTCOUNT for unique order numbers.
  • DAX Measure (Revenue): Calculated as Quantity Sold × Unit Price.
  • Applied interactive filters and slicers for country-level drill-downs.
