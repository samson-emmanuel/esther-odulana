Problem Statement & Analytical Questions

The objective of this analysis is to evaluate road accident data with a focus on casualty impact, severity, and contributing conditions. The dashboard was designed to provide data-driven answers to the following key questions:

Primary KPIs

  • What is the total number of casualties resulting from road accidents?
  • How are casualties distributed by accident severity and vehicle type?
  • What percentage of total casualties does each severity level represent?

Secondary Insights

  • How do monthly casualty trends compare year-over-year?
  • Which road types and surface conditions are associated with the highest casualties?
  • What is the relationship between casualties, area type (urban/rural), and light conditions?
